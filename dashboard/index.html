07

Methodology & Rigor

I

Recall-First Optimization

Objective function prioritizes recall ≥ 0.75 — missing a churner is costlier than a false positive in B2B SaaS.

II

Leakage-Proof Design

Automated AUC scanning detects post-outcome feature leakage before it corrupts model training.

III

Bayesian HPO

Optuna-driven hyperparameter optimization with Tree-structured Parzen Estimators across all model families.

IV

Calibrated Probabilities

Isotonic regression calibration ensures predicted probabilities reflect true churn likelihood — critical for threshold-based business rules.
